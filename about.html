The taste of Nepal – in the heart of Copenhagen

At Lamfuz, it's about more than food - it's about the experience. We bring the authentic Nepalese flavors to Denmark with love, spices and a deep respect for our food culture. Our dishes are inspired by childhood meals in the rice fields of Nepal, where the scents of garlic, ginger and chili filled the air.

Our cuisine is not just about recipes – it’s about craftsmanship, tradition and the hospitality that is the hallmark of Nepal. Each dish is created with precision and respect for the ingredients we work with. From the deep, aromatic spices to the fresh vegetables and slow-cooked sauces, we strive to bring you the same warmth and authenticity that we grew up with.

Here in Copenhagen, we share that tradition with you – served with modern finesse, local ingredients and genuine warmth.

Experience the authentic taste of Nepal in Lamfuz

Lamfuz means slendrian, but with an interior built by the chef himself from recycled materials and with every detail taken care of, you can clearly feel the love Krishna puts into his restaurant. From the uncompromising choice of specialty items to the homemade and perfectly roasted spice blends, there is no doubt that Krishna's ambition is to place Nepal firmly on the Copenhagen dining scene as a unique experience.

On the shelf, Nepalese tea – known for its special softness and umami taste – stands side by side with Danish-produced sodas, and in the virtual room you will find only the best cold-pressed rapeseed oils. The dishes are designed based on Danish seasonal ingredients and shaped with Krishna's sense of Nepal, full of stories. On the plates in Lamfuz you are introduced to a modern story about the Nepalese taste palette in warm and cheerful surroundings.

About Lamfuz

Lamfuz would like to introduce you to the fireworks of flavors that the Nepalese cuisine of our childhood offers. We offer dine-in and takeout, inviting you into our universe full of tasty and healthy relaxation!

Cumin, turmeric, chili, coriander, garlic and ginger bring the food to life and give your taste buds a rollercoaster ride – and it’s healthy too! Lamfuz is for those of you who crave exotic food and new flavors with vegetarian and vegan options. And don’t worry if you’re a dedicated carnivore – we also have menus with lamb, chicken and seasonal fish.
